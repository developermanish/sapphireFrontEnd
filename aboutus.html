How We Are Helping



SAPPHIRE, a student organization under the aegis of Division of Student Welfare, Lovely Professional University, is an amalgamation of smart working, fun loving, innovative students who work towards making sure that students have an ambience that has an aura of innovativeness. We incorporate within ourselves the mentality of thinking big, finding new and enjoyable ways of cracking academics, pushing our limits and finding new horizons to our personalities.
We organize events which helps the students to see, examine and improve the mental, physical and social aspects of their life. If you are the one who wants to be not someone else but the best version of yourself, if you are seeking your horizons and want to break out your edges, we are here to stand, walk and run on that path to your better self.
Our motto is to take everyone together and march ahead, exactly as our slogan says-
ONE TEAM ONE DREAM
